Simple Quiz App

In today's digital world, learning is becoming simpler with the help of technology. Introducing an easy-to-use quiz app designed for teachers and students.

Features

  • Teachers can create quizzes with different question types like multiple-choice or short answer.
  • Students can log in, take the quiz, and get their results instantly.
  • Results are also sent directly to the teacher.
  • This app encourages communication between teachers, students, and parents.
  • Education becomes more transparent and collaborative.

In summary, this quiz app simplifies learning by making assessment easy for both teachers and students.
